About

Piano

Michal Sobkowiak

Born in Poland, graduated from Frederyk Chopin University of Music. He is jury member at PTNA, the International Chopin Piano Competition in ASIA and the jury chairman of European International Piano Concours in Japan. In 2021, he founded the Japan Jazz Pop Piano Competition. In May 2022, he performed with Kanagawa Philharmonic Orchestra for a charity concert for Ukraine.

Bass

Danny Stewart

Scottish born bassist/composer. Toured/recorded with Ayub Ogada (Real World Records) jazz trumpeter Kevin Davy (Lamb/D-semble), and Grammy nominated guitarist David Becker. Co-wrote with Soul artist Jehlisa Anderson (Warner Bros), composed for television, multimedia and game titles (Konami and Sega). Currently runs Bass Japan Direct in addition to doing sessions / composing.

Drums

Dean Thompson

Dean is first and foremost a percussionist, who spent many years teaching drums and touring Australia as a professional musician playing Jazz and Funk primarily, but also other amalgams of contemporary music, such as, Latin, Electronica and World Music. During his time in Australia he played, cowrote and recorded music with Josue Vilches, and played with the video game jazz cover band The Consouls. Now Dean lives in Tokyo and is gigging with world renowned musicians such as Michal Sobkowiak, Harvey Thompson and David Becker.
