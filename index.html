About

Michal Sobkowiak

Born in an artistic family in Poland, Michal Sobkowiak made his TV debut on "Akademia muzyczna" series as the most talented young pianist at the age of ten. After graduated from Chopin Academy of Music (currently known as the Frederyk Chopin University of Music) in 1998, he further pursued his postgraduate study at the ZÃ¼rich Conservatory. Currently, he serves as a professor at Fukushima College and as a lecturer at Showa University of Music. He has been regularly invited as a jury member at PTNA and the International Chopin Piano Competition in ASIA, as well as the jury chairman of European International Piano Concours in Japan. In 2021, he founded the Japan Jazz Pop Piano Competition. In May 2022 he performed with Kanagawa Philharmonic Orchestra for charity concert for Ukraina.

Dean Thompson

Dean is first and foremost a percussionist, who spent many years teaching drums and touring Australia as a professional musician playing Jazz and Funk primarily, but also other amalgams of contemporary music, such as, Latin, Electronica and World Music. During his time in Australia he played, cowrote and recorded music with Josue Vilches, and played with the video game jazz cover band The Consouls. Now Dean lives in Tokyo and is gigging with world renowned musicians such as Michal Sobkowiak, Harvey Thompson and David Becker.

Danny Stewart

Danny is not only a Bassist, he is also skilled as a composer and arranger. He has completed work for many clients including BBC, Nickelodeon, British Embassy, Konami, Capcom, British Telecom and many more smaller entities.
